## Overview

A financial account is required to receive payouts from Polar. We support Stripe Connect accounts, which handle the infrastructure for receiving payments worldwide.

## Account Types

Polar currently supports **Stripe Connect** accounts as the payment processor. Stripe handles:

- Payment processing
- Currency conversion
- Bank transfers
- Compliance requirements

## Setting Up Your Account

### Initial Setup

1. Navigate to your organization's finance settings
2. Click "Create Account" or "Connect Stripe Account"
3. Select your account country from the [supported countries](/merchant-of-record/supported-countries)
4. Complete the Stripe onboarding process

### Account Statuses

Your account will progress through several statuses:

- **Created**: Account record created, onboarding not started
- **Onboarding Started**: Stripe onboarding flow initiated
- **Under Review**: Account submitted and being reviewed (see [Account Reviews](/merchant-of-record/account-reviews))
- **Active**: Account fully operational and can receive payouts
- **Denied**: Account rejected during review

<Warning>
Your account must be in **Active** status with payouts enabled before you can receive funds.
</Warning>

## Account Capabilities

Your Stripe account has three key capabilities that determine what you can do:

### Details Submitted

Indicates whether you've completed the required onboarding information.

### Charges Enabled

When enabled, your account can accept payments from customers.

### Payouts Enabled

When enabled, funds can be transferred to your bank account. This requires:

- Completed onboarding
- Verified identity
- Connected bank account
- Active account status

## Account Administration

### Account Admin

Each financial account has a designated admin who:

- Owns the Stripe account
- Must have verified identity with Stripe
- Can access the Stripe Express dashboard
- Is responsible for compliance

### Changing the Admin

<Warning>
The Stripe account must be deleted before changing the admin. This is required for compliance.
</Warning>

To change the account admin:

1. Delete the existing Stripe connection
2. Assign the new admin (must be an organization member)
3. New admin must have verified identity status
4. Reconnect a new Stripe account

## Multi-Organization Accounts

A single financial account can be linked to multiple organizations, allowing you to:

- Consolidate payouts across organizations
- Simplify financial management
- Reduce administrative overhead

## Billing Information

For reverse invoicing (when Polar invoices you for platform fees), configure:

- **Billing Name**: Legal entity name
- **Billing Address**: Complete business address
- **Additional Info**: Tax ID or other required information
- **Billing Notes**: Optional notes for invoices

## Stripe Express Dashboard

Access your Stripe Express dashboard to:

- View transaction history
- Update bank account details
- Manage business information
- Review payout schedules
- Download Stripe reports

Click "Open Stripe Dashboard" from your account settings to generate a secure login link.

## Account Deletion

Deleting your Stripe account:

1. Removes Stripe account connection
2. Clears all Stripe-related data
3. Disables charges and payouts
4. Archives the account record
5. Allows reconnecting a new account

<Warning>
Deleting your Stripe account is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure all pending payouts are processed first.
</Warning>

## Disconnecting vs Deleting

### Disconnect

- Breaks the link to Stripe
- Preserves historical data
- Allows reconnecting the same account
- Account remains in Polar's system

### Delete

- Permanently removes Stripe account
- Deletes account from Stripe's system
- Cannot be reconnected
- Use when starting fresh

## Security

- Account access controlled by organization membership
- Admin actions require authentication
- Stripe handles PCI compliance
- All financial data encrypted at rest and in transit
