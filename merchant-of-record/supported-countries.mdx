## Customer Support

### Global Customer Reach

Customers can purchase from anywhere in the world that Stripe supports payments, which includes:

- All seller-supported countries listed above
- Additional countries where Stripe processes payments
- 135+ currencies

### Payment Methods

Supported payment methods vary by country but generally include:

- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, etc.
- **Digital Wallets**: Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Bank Transfers**: ACH, SEPA, etc.
- **Local Payment Methods**: Region-specific options

## Currency Support

Polar supports 135+ currencies through Stripe:

### Major Currencies

- USD (United States Dollar)
- EUR (Euro)
- GBP (British Pound)
- JPY (Japanese Yen)
- CAD (Canadian Dollar)
- AUD (Australian Dollar)
- CHF (Swiss Franc)
- CNY (Chinese Yuan)
- INR (Indian Rupee)
- SGD (Singapore Dollar)

### Zero-Decimal Currencies

Some currencies have special handling (no decimal places):

- ISK (Icelandic Króna)
- HUF (Hungarian Forint)
- TWD (New Taiwan Dollar)
- UGX (Ugandan Shilling)

### Currency Conversion

- Automatic conversion between customer and seller currencies
- Exchange rates provided by Stripe
- Typical spread: ~1% above mid-market rate
- Rate determined at transaction time

## Limitations by Country

### Restricted Countries

Some countries are not supported due to:

- US sanctions
- Banking restrictions
- Regulatory complexity
- Stripe availability

### Country-Specific Requirements

Some countries require:

- Additional verification documents
- Local business registration
- Tax registration numbers
- Specific account types

### First Payout Delays

Some regions may have:

- Extended verification periods
- Longer payout processing times
- Additional compliance checks
- Limitations on initial transaction volumes

## Payout Processing Times

Payout timing varies by country and banking system:

### Fast Regions (2-3 business days)

- United States
- United Kingdom
- Most of Europe (SEPA)
- Australia

### Standard Regions (3-7 business days)

- Canada
- Asia Pacific (varies)
- Latin America (varies)
- Middle East (varies)

### First Payout

First payout may take longer:

- Additional 7-14 days for verification
- Bank account validation
- Identity confirmation
- Compliance checks

## Compliance Requirements

### Know Your Customer (KYC)

All sellers must complete:

- Identity verification
- Business information (if applicable)
- Bank account verification
- Tax information

### Country-Specific Compliance

Depending on location, you may need:

- Business license
- Tax registration number
- Proof of address
- Additional documentation

### Ongoing Compliance

- Regular account reviews
- Transaction monitoring
- Updated documentation requests
- Compliance with local regulations
